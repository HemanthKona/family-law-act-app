Apply for Family Order

This service will help you fill out forms needed to apply for FAMILY LAW ORDERS in Provincial Court. You can use this service to apply to the court for family law orders about:

  • Protection from family violence
  • Family Law Matters including parenting arrangements, child support, contact with a child, guardianship of a child and spousal support under the Family Law Act
  • Case Management
  • Priority Parenting Matters
  • Relocation of a child
  • Enforcement of agreements and court orders under the Family Law Act
